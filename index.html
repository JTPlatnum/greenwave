We are Green Wave Farms

We are a grower-owned, vertically integrated produce company that specializes in value-added products for retail and foodservice companies.

Farm to the Table

We cater to companies who find that forging direct relationships with their growers gives them superior quality. We offer safe produce, flexibility of product choice, as well as custom specifications that only comes from dealing directly with the grower.

From Our Grower to You

Our direct relationships also benefit our growers, allowing them the flexibility to work in creative ways with our customers, a process often impossible in a large commercial agribusiness environment.

Focused on Your Health

Our food safety and facility management team boasts over seventy-five years in the produce business. We are Primus certified from the field to our processing facility.

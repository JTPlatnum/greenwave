Our Products

Green Wave Farms is a Grower owned company that specializes in value added items for retail and foodservice. We farm over 4,000 acres of Brussels Sprouts, Snow Peas, Sugar Snap Peas, and Green Beans. We are a year round supplier of these items with farms in California and Texas, as well as in Mexico; including Sinaloa, Puebla, Baja California.
